Who We Are

One Block.
One Vision.

BLOK SUPPLY started in 2019 on a single block in Bed-Stuy, Brooklyn with a simple conviction: the streets deserve better clothes. Not faster clothes. Not cheaper clothes. Better ones. We set out to build a brand that treats its customers like adults — no hype, no gimmicks, no artificial scarcity. Just premium streetwear, honestly made and fairly priced.

What Drives Us

Our Three Non-Negotiables

01
Material Integrity

Every single piece we release starts with the fabric. We use 240gsm+ combed cotton for tops, durable cotton-twill blends for bottoms, and YKK hardware on every zipper. If a material doesn't hold up in our tests, it doesn't make the cut.

02
Honest Craftsmanship

We don't do heat-pressed graphics. Every logo is screen-printed or embroidered. Double-stitched hems. Reinforced stress points. We believe clothes should look better after 50 washes, not worse. That's the BLOK standard.

03
No Compromise Design

Our design team in Brooklyn obsesses over every dimension — the drop of a shoulder, the taper of a pant leg, the weight distribution of a jacket. We make clothes that fit correctly without asking you to size up or down to compensate.

How We Work

From Sketch to Street

01

Research

We watch how people actually dress — on the block, on the train, at the gym. Then we design for those real moments, not a mood board.

02

Prototype

Every piece goes through at least four sample rounds. We test fit, fabric behavior, wash durability, and how it looks after six months of regular wear.

03

Produce

We work with a small number of ethical manufacturing partners who share our commitment to fair labor practices and consistent quality across every unit.

04

Deliver

Every order is quality-checked before it ships. Free shipping over $75, free returns within 30 days, and a COD option for every order, everywhere in the US.
